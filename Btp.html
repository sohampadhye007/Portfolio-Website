Design and manufacturing of Amphibious Surveillance Mobile Robot

Key Features

  • Versatile Design: Our team designed a highly versatile amphibious surveillance robot, which could seamlessly transition between different environments, making it suitable for a wide range of applications, including military and environmental monitoring.
  • Remote Control Capability: We implemented remote control functionality, allowing the robot to be operated remotely via mobile devices. This feature enabled real-time surveillance and control, enhancing the safety and efficiency of the robot in various scenarios.
  • GPS Integration: To enhance the robot's surveillance capabilities, we incorporated GPS technology. This enabled precise enemy positioning and tracking during surveillance missions, making it an invaluable tool for reconnaissance operations.
  • Technological Expertise: The project required a diverse skill set and the use of cutting-edge technologies. We utilized composites for the robot's construction, 3D printing for custom components, and applied principles of machine design to ensure robustness and reliability. Additionally, a Raspberry Pi served as the core controller, facilitating data processing and communication.
