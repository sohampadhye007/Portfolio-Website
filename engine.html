Project Details

Intake System Design for Formula Student Racing car

Project information

  • Category: Engine Rebuild
  • Institute: VIT Pune
  • Project date: April 2018

Engine Selection

The project utilizes a compact and race-tuned KTM 390 engine due to its manufacturer-tailored performance, ease of maintenance, and availability of spare parts. The engine's integration with the chassis maintains the stock bike's inclination and provides organized space for engine components.

Engine Rebuild

  • Post-race inspection of the piston block and engine components.
  • Disassembly and replacement of worn or damaged parts.

Valve Lift Analysis:

  • Through experimental measurements, we acquired valve lift data and utilized Ricardo Wave software to create lift diagrams, ultimately fine-tuning the valve lift profiles for improved engine efficiency and power output.
