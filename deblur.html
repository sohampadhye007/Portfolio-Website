Project information

  • Category: M.Tech Major Project

Image deblurring using inertial measurement sensors

  • Novel Algorithm : Created an innovative algorithm for predicting motion blur kernels, enabling the recovery of sharp images..
  • Sensor Utilization: Extracted precise motion data (linear acceleration and angular velocity) from Inertial Measurement Units (IMUs).
  • Interdisciplinary Approach: Leveraged Python, MATLAB, Intel RealSense Depth Camera, and Raspberry Pi for development.
  • Research Paper: Currently authoring a research paper for submission to a flagship conference
